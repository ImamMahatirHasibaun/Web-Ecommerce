Our Story

Crafting elegance since 2010

The Christian Wijaya Legacy

Founded in 2010, Christian Wijaya has established itself as a premier luxury fashion house that seamlessly blends timeless elegance with contemporary sophistication. Our commitment to exceptional craftsmanship and innovative design has made us a respected name in the fashion industry.

Each piece in our collection reflects our dedication to quality and attention to detail, ensuring that our clients experience the true essence of luxury fashion. We believe that style is not just about following trends, but about creating a lasting impression that transcends time.

Our Values

Our Journey

2010

The Beginning

Christian Wijaya founded the brand with a small collection of handcrafted accessories in Jakarta.

2013

First Flagship Store

Opened our first flagship store in Jakarta's Sudirman Business District.

2015

International Expansion

Expanded to Singapore and launched our first international boutique.

2018

Sustainability Initiative

Introduced our first sustainable collection using ethically sourced materials.

2022

2025

Today

Continuing to innovate and expand while staying true to our core values and vision.
